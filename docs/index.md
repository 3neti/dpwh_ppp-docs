# DPWH Public-Private Partnership Documentation

Welcome to the DPWH Public-Private Partnership (PPP) documentation portal. This site contains comprehensive information about PPP projects, processes, and guidelines.

## Overview

The Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH) implements infrastructure projects through Public-Private Partnership arrangements to leverage private sector expertise, efficiency, and resources in delivering public infrastructure and services.

## About PPP

Public-Private Partnerships are contractual agreements between a government agency and a private sector entity. Through PPPs, the skills and assets of each sector (public and private) are shared in delivering a service or facility for the use of the general public.
